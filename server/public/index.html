Organelle Database

plastidDB and mitoDB

plastidDBis a web-based database for plastid DNA, currently it maintained over 1300+ plastid DNA reference sequences and their related biology information, in which 1000+ are chloroplast DNA, also known as cpDNA.

mitoDBis a web-based database for mitochondrion DNA, mtDNA, maintaining over 8000+ reference sequences of mitochondrion DNA and their related biology information.
